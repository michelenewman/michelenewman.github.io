I am a doctoral student in the Information School at the University of Washington, advised by Dr. Jin Ha Lee. I am a member of the [UW Gamer Group](https://gamer.ischool.uw.edu/) and the [Digital Youth Lab](https://digitalyouth.ischool.uw.edu/) working with [KidsTeam](https://www.kidsteam.ischool.uw.edu/) and Dr. Jason Yip. My research interests include the organization and preservation of interactive media, creativity, and human-computer interaction. I'm also a musician and avid gamer, passions that often weave their way into my scholarly pursuits! You can find out more about my research [here](https://michelenewman.github.io//research/). 

Recently, I had the opportunity to talk to UW News about the impact of AI on children's creativity! You can check it out [here](https://www.washington.edu/news/2024/05/29/ai-kids-creativity-chatgpt/).
